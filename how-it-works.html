How Our NYC Bottle Pickup Service Works

Simple, Convenient, and Rewarding

1

Schedule Your Pickup

Call us at 212-473-3687 to schedule a convenient pickup time for your bottles and cans. We serve all five boroughs of NYC: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, and Staten Island.

2

Prepare Your Bottles & Cans

Sort your bottles and cans (plastic with plastic, cans with cans). Count them yourself and mark the quantity on each bag clearly. For glass, limit to 50 pieces per bag. Use transparent bags. Our minimum pickup is 1000 containers. Our drivers will verify the count to ensure fairness.

3

Get Paid in Cash

Our driver will arrive at your location, verify your count of bottles and cans, and pay you cash on the spot. We pay the full current deposit value for each eligible container in New York State.

Why Choose Our NYC Bottle Pickup Service?

Save Time and Effort

No more waiting in lines at redemption centers or making multiple trips to return your bottles and cans. Our service comes directly to your home or business, saving you valuable time and energy.

Environmentally Friendly

By using our service, you're contributing to NYC's recycling efforts and helping reduce waste. Each bottle or can recycled means one less container in landfills or polluting our environment.

Convenient for Businesses

We work with restaurants, bars, offices, and other businesses across NYC to provide regular bottle pickup services. Contact us to set up a recurring schedule that works for your needs.

What Containers Do You Accept?

We accept all New York State deposit bottles and cans, including beer, soda, water, and other beverage containers that have "NY 5¢" printed on them. This includes plastic bottles, glass bottles, and aluminum cans.
