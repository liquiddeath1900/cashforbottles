How Our NYC Bottle Pickup Service Works

Simple, Convenient, and Rewarding

1

Schedule Your Pickup

Call us at 347-749-8146 to schedule a convenient pickup time for your bottles and cans. We serve all five boroughs of NYC: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, and Staten Island.

2

Prepare Your Bottles & Cans

Sort your bottles and cans (plastic with plastic, cans with cans). For glass, limit to 50 pieces per bag. Use transparent bags and mark the number of containers on each bag. Our minimum pickup is 1000 containers.

3

Get Paid in Cash

Our driver will arrive at your location, verify your count of bottles and cans, and pay you cash on the spot. We pay the full current deposit value for each eligible container in New York State.
