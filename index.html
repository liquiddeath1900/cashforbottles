Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We serve all boroughs of NYC including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and Bronx.

Is there a minimum for pickup?

Yes, we require a minimum of 1000 containers for our pickup service.

How do I prepare my bottles and cans?

Sort plastic bottles with plastic and cans with cans. For glass, no more than 50 pieces per bag. Please use transparent bags and write the number of pieces on the bag clearly. For best results, write the amount on a small piece of cardboard and place it inside the bag where it's easily visible for the pickup driver to spot.
