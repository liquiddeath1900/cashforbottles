Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you pay per bottle/can?
We pay the full redemption value of 5¢ per eligible container as set by New York state law.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all boroughs of NYC including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, and Staten Island.
Is there a minimum for pickup?
Yes, we require a minimum of 100 containers for our pickup service.
How do I prepare my bottles and cans?
Simply rinse containers and keep them in bags or boxes. We'll handle the sorting!
